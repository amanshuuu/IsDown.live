About IsDown.live

IsDown.live is a free, real-time service status checker that monitors 70+ popular services including Spotify, GitHub, Netflix, Discord, AWS, ChatGPT, and more. We check every service every 60 seconds and instantly surface outages, degradation, and slowdowns.

Wondering "is it down for everyone or just me?" — IsDown.live answers that instantly. See live user reports, uptime history, and response time charts for every service. No account needed, no ads, works on all devices.

Set up instant email or push notifications so you're the first to know when your favourite service recovers. IsDown.live is the fastest way to check if Spotify, GitHub, Netflix, Discord, or any other service is down right now.
